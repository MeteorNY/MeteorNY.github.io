Welcome

This is the GitHub organization page for the MeteorNY Meetup, a regular meeting of MeteorJS enthusiasts in the Greater New York City Area. The goal of the organization is to support the development of distributed web applications using the MeteorJS platform by connecting people with diverse backgrounds (artists, designers, developers, entrepreneurs) and having collaborative and conversational efforts to improve our understanding and usage of this platform.

About MeteorJS

"Meteor is an open-source platform for building top-quality web apps in a fraction of the time, whether you're an expert developer or just getting started." To get started with learning Meteor, visit the MeteorJS Website or browse the community-maintained Meteorpedia to find a wealth of resources for self-study.
